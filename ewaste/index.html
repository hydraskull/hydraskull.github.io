What is Fray-x?

A solution to an inconvenience

Fray-X is a superior MacBook MagSafe Power Adapter fray prevention technology. It protects the weakest joint of the adapter by keeping the cord from bending at severe angles. Fray-X is more cost efficient and effective than other fray prevention methods, and will save you from buying new, expensive power adapters. It is easy to install and works with both new and previously damaged MagSafe power adapters.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Fray-X work?

Fray-X protects the weakest joint on MacBook MagSafe Power Adapters: where the cord meets the brick. It keeps the cord from bending at the joint, instead allowing the cord to bend above Fray-X. Fray-X also forces the cord to make a smooth bend around the curved top to prevent the cord from simply fraying further up the cord. It eliminates the cord’s ability to make sharp, damaging bends and keeps the joint at the brick from bending at all.

Why choose Fray-X?

Buying a new power adapter is expensive and time consuming. Instead, Fray-X can greatly extend the life of your power adapter for a fraction of the price of a new one. Fray-X is also more affordable and effective than leading market competitors. Additionally, it is a hassle free installation that takes less than a minute.

How do you install Fray-X?

Fray-X is easy and fast to install. Simply push the cord into the slot, and stick it to the brick. There are instructions on the package and a video “How to Install Fray-X” with more detailed instructions and a demonstration of Fray-X being installed.

Can Fray-X be removed?

Fray-X can be removed, but once removed the tape will lose it’s stickiness and it cannot be put back on without getting new tape. To remove Fray-X, first wiggle it back and forth to to unstick the the tape. Once it is no longer stuck to the brick, slowly pull the cord out, starting at the wider end of the slot at the bottom of Fray-X.

What power adapters does Fray-X work on?

Fray-X works on all current MacBook MagSafe Power Adapters. This includes 85, 60, and 45 watt power adapters. The computers that use these chargers include MacBook Pros and MacBook Airs of all versions and screen sizes.

Will Fray-X help if my cord is already frayed?

Installing Fray-X on an already damaged cord will greatly slow the the spread of the damage, but not completely stop it. It is best to install Fray-X before power adapters have a chance to start fraying. If installing on a previously damaged cord, wrap the damage with a small piece of tape to cover exposed wires. Only use enough tape to cover the damage, as any more tape will result in Fray-X not fitting properly.
